The World through NASA lens

Welcome to "The World through NASA lens" where you will be able to see the best picture clicked by NASA satellites from around the Universe.
The Astronomy Picture of the Day (APOD) is a NASA project that provides a daily image or photograph of our universe.
It showcases a wide range of images, including photos of planets, stars, galaxies, nebulae, and other astronomical phenomena,
as well as significant events captured by NASA missions and observatories.
